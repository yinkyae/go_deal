    Product Description
    The iPhone 7, with its amazing features and drool-worthy design, looks every bit as efficient as it is. With a powerful processor and ample internal storage, this smartphone offers you a seamless performance.
    Design

    The iPhone 7 features a unibody design that is easy to hold. Bead-blasted aluminum is used to give this phone's body an elegant matte finish. The enclosure of the phone is water and splash resistant, so you don't have to worry about spills, splashes and dust.

    Home Button

    The Home button is also re-engineered to be more responsive, durable and pressure sensitive. It works in tandem with the Taptic Engine to provide you with precise tactile feedback every time. The advanced Touch ID fingerprint sensor makes using your phone safer, easier and faster.

    Cameras

    Featuring a six-element lens, an ƒ/1.8 aperture and a Quad-LED True Tone flash, the 12MP primary camera of this smartphone lets you shoot excellent photos and up to 4K videos, even in low-light conditions. The rear camera also features wide color capture and optical image stabilization, so your Live Photos and regular images look more vivid and vibrant. The 7MP FaceTime HD camera, with Retina Flash and wide color capture, allows you to take beautiful, portrait-worthy selfies. The iPhone 7 is equipped with slo-mo support in 1080p and 720p HD.

    Advanced Image Signal Processor

    The A10 Fusion chip of the iPhone 7 is equipped with an Apple-designed image signal processor that powers over 100 billion operations to make your photos look more beautiful.

    Photos App

    Organize and share your photos in an easier and better way with the Photos app. It will sort your photos and videos into albums based on your preferences.

    Retina HD Display

    The 11.93 cm Retina HD display brings your images to life. It features 3D Touch, which makes the touchscreen more intuitive and responsive.

    A10 Fusion Chip

    Thanks to the four-core design, the CPU of the A10 Fusion chip of this smartphone offers you a powerful performance, making multitasking a breeze. It also boosts your battery's life, so it can keep up with your active lifestyle.

    Storage

    This smartphone comes with 32GB internal storage capacity to offer you more room for all your favorite video clips, songs, applications and high-definition photos.

    Crisp Audio

    With powerful stereo speakers, the iPhone 7 offers you an immersive audio experience. The EarPods of this smartphone have a Lightning connector. You can also use the 3.5 mm headphone jack adapter when the need arises.

    LTE Advanced

    This smartphone supports VoLTE (high-quality wideband calls) to enhance the quality of your calls. It also allows you to answer calls over a Wi-Fi connection.

    iOS 13

    Apart from introducing a bold look, the iOS 13 provides major updates to apps that are used every day, new ways to safeguard your privacy, and improvements across the entire system that give you a faster and more delightful iPhone experience.
